Question 5: Water Retention

How does particle size affect water retention in soil?

  • Large particles (like gravel and sand):
    • Allow water to flow through easily
    • Retain very little water
  • Small particles (like clay):
    • Have larger surface area
    • Resist water flow
    • Retain more water

Question 6: Topsoil

Why is topsoil ideal for plant growth?

Topsoil is ideal because:
  • Contains a mixture of different-sized particles
  • Retains just the right amount of water
  • Creates a nutrient-rich environment
  • Provides optimal conditions for plant growth
